A PAW FOR A CAUSE

Helping hands for the dogs

By: J.Rubio, N.Cayanan & K.Castro

Living better by serving the homeless tame animals and giving them love and care they deserve. We, the founder, established an organization named "A Paw for a Cause". 

Our goals and purpose are:

  • To provide tame animals as much freedom and comfort they need
  • To save animals from the heartless society
  • The shelter exists to serve animals and caring for the animals and always will be the main priority
  • To make them giggle with glee ; treated with respect and love
  • To ensure that every animal is as happy as it can possibly be

A Paw for a Cause is a non-profit organization. It is located in City of Malolos, Bulacan and also the first animal shelter in our city that is still functioning today. All animals are fed only by premium dog or cat food. Our next goal is to upgrade the facilities and to continue providing the highest quality of care to the animals in the shelter.
